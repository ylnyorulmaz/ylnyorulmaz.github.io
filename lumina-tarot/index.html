Project

🌓 Lumina Tarot

Minimalist AI tarot for reflection — not fortune-telling.

Live AI PWA Free
Open Lumina ↗ ← Back to portfolio

Why I built it

Most tarot apps are ad-heavy, data-hungry, and often emotionally manipulative. Lumina explores a different direction: treating tarot as a symbolic narrative system for introspection rather than supernatural prediction.

It does not claim to predict the future. It is a mirror tool powered by generative AI, designed to help users interpret symbols through language.

An experiment in

  • AI-assisted symbolic interpretation
  • Minimalist product design
  • Ethical emotional UX

How it works

  1. User draws 3 symbolic cards.
  2. AI interprets cards contextually using structured prompts.
  3. Output includes Roots, Present energy, Forward direction, and a mantra.

Generated text is designed to feel personal while remaining psychologically safe.

UX decisions

  • No login required
  • No daily limit gating
  • No fake urgency
  • No “unlock your destiny” prompts

Core philosophy

Three principles that shape every decision in the product.

Symbol over superstition

Archetypes, not prophecy. Tarot works as a reflective framework, not a prediction engine.

Guidance over determinism

Reflection, not dependency. The goal is clarity, not an oracle to defer to.

Minimal interface, maximal clarity

No upsell traps, no distraction. Every element earns its place on screen.

What I learned

  • Emotional tone in AI prompts matters more than structure
  • Small UX choices shape trust significantly
  • Minimalism increases perceived depth
  • Analytics matters as much as creativity

Future directions

  • Journal mode
  • Save and revisit readings
  • AI-guided self-reflection mode
  • Archetype deep-dive pages for SEO

Try a reading

Lumina Tarot is not about predicting what will happen. It is about helping you articulate what you already feel.

Sometimes, the right words are more powerful than the right answers.
