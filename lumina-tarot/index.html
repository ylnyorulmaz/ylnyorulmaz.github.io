🌓 Lumina Tarot

Minimalist AI Tarot Experience — Built for Reflection, Not Fortune-Telling

Lumina Tarot is a lightweight, distraction-free web experience that transforms symbolic tarot archetypes into reflective AI-generated guidance.

It does not claim to predict the future. It is not mystical authority. It is a mirror tool powered by generative AI, designed to help users interpret symbols through language.

Built as a fast, privacy-respecting, no-signup PWA, Lumina focuses on emotional clarity rather than entertainment noise.

Why I Built It

Most tarot apps today are ad-heavy, data-hungry, clickbait-driven, and often emotionally manipulative.

Lumina explores a different direction: treating tarot as a symbolic narrative system for introspection rather than supernatural prediction.

Lumina is an experiment in

  • AI-assisted symbolic interpretation
  • Minimalist product design
  • Ethical emotional UX

Core Philosophy

  1. Symbol over superstition: archetypes, not prophecy.
  2. Guidance over determinism: reflection, not dependency.
  3. Minimal interface, maximal clarity: no upsell traps.

How It Works

  • User draws 3 symbolic cards.
  • AI interprets cards contextually using structured prompts.
  • Output includes Roots, Present energy, Forward direction, and a mantra.

Generated text is designed to feel personal while remaining psychologically safe.

UX Decisions

  • No login required
  • No daily limit gating
  • No fake urgency
  • No manipulative "unlock your destiny" prompts

What I Learned

  • Emotional tone in AI prompts matters more than structure.
  • Small UX choices shape trust.
  • Minimalism increases perceived depth.
  • Analytics matters as much as creativity.

Future Directions

  • Journal mode
  • Save and revisit readings
  • AI-guided self-reflection mode
  • Archetype deep-dive static pages for SEO
