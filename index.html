About

I am not interested in performing entrepreneurship or adding AI to everything. I like finding the smallest version of an idea that can prove whether anyone cares.

That produces an unusual body of work: security tools, strategy games, decision rituals, developer products and small consumer utilities. The categories change. The method does not—define the real problem, remove the theatre, build the core and put it in front of people.

This site is the working index of that process.
