The format

Each game focuses on a different sport. Each challenge follows the same simple format:

  • One athlete
  • Six progressive hints
  • Four matches per day
  • No spoilers
  • No ads
  • No accounts required

The philosophy

We believe sports are more interesting when you remember the details.

The transfers. The rivalries. The forgotten finals. The tactical eras.

...erly is not about trivia speed. It’s about recognition. A small daily ritual for serious fans.
