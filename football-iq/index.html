The format

Each game focuses on a different sport. Every challenge follows the same simple structure:

  • One athlete
  • Six progressive hints
  • Four matches per day
  • No spoilers
  • No ads
  • No accounts required

The philosophy

Sports are more interesting when you remember the details.

The transfers. The rivalries. The forgotten finals. The tactical eras.

...erly is not about trivia speed. It’s about recognition — a small daily ritual for serious fans.

Why daily?

Because anticipation matters. One puzzle resets every 24 hours. You can’t binge it. You can’t grind it. Come back tomorrow.

Built different

No login walls. No pop-ups. No dopamine traps. Just you and the clues.

If you watch full races, remember the assists, or know who finished second in 2012 — you’ll enjoy this.
